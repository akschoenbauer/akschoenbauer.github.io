A little about me:

I am from Fort Collins, Colorado. True to the CO Native stereotype, I love being outdoors and getting into the mountains to hike and explore whenever possible.

I'm in my final semester here at CU. I am a Strategic Communications - Media Design major with a minor in CTD.

In my free time, I love creating and working with my hands. Some of my favorite hobbies are cooking, baking, and crafting with fiber, wood, metal, and other materials.

My favorite time of day is the morning - I love opening my windows to let the sun in and enjoying the fresh and quiet moments. I start each morning with a yoga practice and a mug of coffee, which always sets me up to have a great day.
